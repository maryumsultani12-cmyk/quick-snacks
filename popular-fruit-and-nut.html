Fruit & Nut Energy Bites

★★★★★ 4.6 (192) 96 Reviews • 4 Photos

Fruit & Nut Energy Bites are sweet, chewy, and naturally energizing.
Made with dried fruits, nuts, and seeds — perfect for quick snacks, workouts, or healthy cravings on the go.

Watch Recipe Video

Prep Time

15 mins

Cook Time

0 mins

Total Time

15 mins

Servings

10 Bites

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dates (pitted)
  • ½ cup almonds or walnuts
  • ¼ cup peanuts or cashews
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp oats
  • Optional: chia seeds or cocoa powder

Instructions

Add dates and nuts to a food processor.
Blend until mixture becomes sticky.
Add peanut butter, honey, and oats.
Mix well and roll into small balls.
Chill for 10 minutes and enjoy.
