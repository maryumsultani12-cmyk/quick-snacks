Snowman Cheesecake Cups
Ingredients
  • Cream cheese
  • Fresh cream
  • Sugar
  • Chocolate biscuits (crushed)
  • Chocolate chips / candy (decoration)
Description

Cream cheese is blended with sugar and fresh cream until smooth. The mixture is layered with crushed chocolate biscuits in serving cups. This dessert provides calcium and energy while offering a creamy and satisfying taste.

Reindeer Pancakes
Ingredients
  • Pancake batter
  • Chocolate spread
  • Banana slices
  • Pretzels
  • Candy eyes
Description

Mini pancakes are prepared from a smooth batter using flour, eggs, milk, and a small amount of sugar. The butter is gently mixed to achieve a light and even consistency. Chocolate spread, fresh fruits, and edible toppings are used to create the reindeer design. This dish provides quick energy, essential carbohydrates, and natural nutrients from fruits.
