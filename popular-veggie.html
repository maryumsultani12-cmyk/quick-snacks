Veggie & Hummus Cups

★★★★★ 4.6 (82) 74 Reviews • 3 Photos

Veggie & Hummus Cups are fresh, colorful, and super healthy snacks.
Packed with crunchy vegetables and creamy hummus — perfect for parties, lunch boxes, or guilt-free evening munching.

Watch Recipe Video

Prep Time

15 mins

Cook Time

0 mins

Total Time

15 mins

Servings

4 cups

Ingredients

  • 1 cup hummus (classic or flavored)
  • Carrot sticks
  • Cucumber slices
  • Bell pepper strips
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Olives (optional)
  • Fresh parsley or paprika for garnish

Instructions

Wash and cut all vegetables into bite-size sticks.
Spoon hummus evenly into small serving cups.
Arrange veggies upright inside the hummus.
Garnish with parsley or paprika.
Serve fresh or chill before serving.
