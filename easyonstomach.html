Broccoli and Boiled Egg Salad
Ingredients
  • Broccoli
  • Boiled eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
Description

This dish is prepared using fresh broccoli florets, boiled eggs, carrots, and light seasoning such as olive oil and herbs. The vegetables are lightly steamed, and the eggs are sliced and mixed gently to maintain texture. It is rich in protein, fiber, and vitamins, which support muscle strength and digestive health.

Nutty Banana
Ingredients
  • Ripe banana – 1
  • Nuts (almonds, walnuts, peanuts) – 2 tbsp
  • Yogurt or milk – ½ cup

  • Cinnamon – pinch (optional)
Description

This dish contains almonds, pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, dried fruits, and raisins. All ingredients are mixed raw or lightly roasted to enhance natural flavor. It is a nutrient-dense snack that improves brain function, boosts immunity, and provides long-lasting energy.
