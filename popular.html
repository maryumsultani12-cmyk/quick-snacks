Avocado Toast Deluxe

★★★★★ 4.6 (82) 68 Reviews • 2 Photos

Avocado Toast Deluxe is creamy, crunchy, and healthy. Made with ripe avocados, toasted bread,
fresh veggies, and light seasoning — perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Watch Recipe Video

Prep Time

10 mins

Cook Time

5 mins

Total Time

15 mins

Servings

2

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt & black pepper (to taste)
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Optional: egg, feta cheese, seeds

Instructions

Toast bread until golden brown.
Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt & pepper.
Spread avocado mixture on toast.
Add tomatoes and toppings of choice.
Serve fresh and enjoy.
